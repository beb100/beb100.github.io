Pennsylvania State Police

The Pennsylvania State Police (PSP) is one of the earliest statewide law enforcement agencies in the United States. PSP is responsible for highway patrol, criminal investigations, and public safety services across the Commonwealth. This website provides a simple overview of the PA State Police, including trooper salary and benefits, and a closer look at daily life at the PSP Academy.

About the PA State Police

PSP was established in 1905 and has grown into a modern law enforcement agency with specialized units, advanced technology, and a strong commitment to serving local communities. Troopers are trained to handle everything from traffic enforcement and crash investigations to emergency response and major criminal cases.
