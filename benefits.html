Cadet Salary & Benefits

The Pennsylvania State Police offer competitive pay and strong benefits for cadets and newly appointed troopers. Below is a breakdown of salary, time off, health coverage, and additional incentives.

Salary Overview

Category Details
Cadet Salary $1,500–$1,600 bi-weekly
Trooper Starting Salary $66,000–$70,000 per year
Health Insurance Medical, dental, and vision included
Retirement State pension and deferred compensation options
Paid Time Off Vacation, holidays, sick days

Cadet Requirements

  • Must be at least 20 years old at application
  • High school diploma or GED
  • U.S. citizen
  • Valid driver’s license
  • Must pass written, physical, medical, and background checks
