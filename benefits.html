Pennsylvania State Police

Trooper Salary & Benefits Overview

Base Salary & Earnings

Position / Timeframe Annual Base Salary (Starting) Notes
After Academy Graduation (Trooper) $71,647 Starting base salary.
After ~5 Years of Service ~$96,225 Base pay increase after years of service.

Other Benefits & Allowances

Benefit Type Details
Clothing Allowance $600/year for uniform/clothing maintenance after becoming Trooper.
Vacation, Sick, Holidays, Personal Days • 10 vacation days 1st year, then 15 days starting 2nd year.
• 15 sick days / year.
• 13 paid holidays / year.
• 4 personal days / year.
Health, Dental, Vision, Prescription Coverage After graduation/promoted to Trooper — Trooper and eligible dependents receive full coverage paid by Commonwealth.
Life Insurance Group life insurance available after 90 days of employment; cadets eligible, benefit equivalent to annual salary.
Veterans Preference / Education Benefits Veterans preference points and eligibility to use the GI Bill during Academy training and probationary period.

Note: Benefit amounts and entitlements are subject to change according to current labor contracts and agency policies — always confirm with a recruiter for up‑to‑date info.
